About Us

At Wings To Sawa, we are passionate about delivering an unforgettable dining experience centered around one thing—delicious, flavorful, and unlimited wings. Located in the heart of Binangonan, Rizal, our restaurant was built with one goal in mind: to create a place where friends, families, and groups can bond over great food and great moments.

We offer a diverse lineup of wing flavors ranging from sweet, spicy, tangy, and savory. Whether you're a fan of Mango Habanero, Garlic Parmesan, or prefer something adventurous, we make sure there's something for every taste.

Our Promise

✔ Fresh and flavorful wings every day
✔ Safe and clean dining environment
✔ Affordable and sulit pricing at ₱315.00 per head
✔ Friendly and fast service (we continuously improve based on customer feedback!)

More than just unlimited wings, Wings To Sawa is about building memories—8 servings shared with barkada, celebrations with family, and laugh-filled moments around the table. Every visit is crafted to make you feel at home.

Our Mission

To serve high-quality wings at affordable prices while creating a welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy good food and good company.

Our Vision

To become one of Rizal’s top comfort food destinations, known for flavor, value, and a dining experience that keeps customers coming back.
